**Knowledge Infrastructure for AI**

---

We're building knowledge infrastructure for the AI era — KBaaS (Knowledge Base as a Service). Our mission is to empower every AI Agent with instant access to a personal, pluggable knowledge base.

---

## What We Offer

**One-Click Build**
Upload your private documents and we'll build you a dedicated knowledge base. No complex setup needed — just upload and you get production-grade retrieval.

**Instant Subscribe**
Subscribe to our public knowledge bases covering Study, Work, and Life. They're continuously updated and ready to use.

**API Access**
Integrate with our RESTful API or MCP protocol. Any AI application can pull knowledge context whenever needed.
